### Job Description
### Data Specialist: part-time (50% FTE)
<br>

### The Company

PrediX is a medical startup developing an AI-driven virtual biopsy
platform to help revolutionize cancer care. By enabling faster, more
precise, and less invasive diagnostics, we aim to improve patient
outcomes while reducing costs.

Current patient diagnosis and management methods are invasive, slow and
costly, often leading to delayed treatment and mortality. PrediX
transforms this process by providing rapid, precise and non-invasive
AI-powered diagnostics, using proprietary multimodal algorithms. Our
cutting-edge platform will accelerate the entire patient journey, from
initial detection to follow-up care, improving patient outcomes and
significantly reducing costs.

### The Role

We are seeking a Data Specialist to support the development of PrediX's
radiology-pathology database and help transform complex clinical data
into structured, high-quality datasets for AI development.

You will play a central role in case pairing, data curation, metadata
organization, quality control, and workflow automation. This role is
ideal for someone who combines **clinical orientation with structured
data thinking and practical technical ability.**

This is a hybrid, part-time position (50% FTE), reporting to the CTO.

**Location:** Ramat Gan\
**Department:** R&D / Data\
**Start date:** Immediate

### Key Responsibilities

#### Clinical Data Curation and Pairing

- Review radiology and pathology reports, metadata, and related clinical
  information to support case-level pairing and annotation.
- Build, maintain, and update structured case records linking imaging,
  reports, pathology, and metadata into consistent, traceable datasets.
- Maintain and update the company SQL database, including creating,
  editing, correcting, and validating case-level records and metadata to
  support reliable downstream research use.
- Apply structured logic to identify matches, inconsistencies, missing
  data, and quality issues across datasets.

#### Workflow Automation and Data Quality

- Turn repetitive manual workflows into repeatable queries, scripts,
  checks, templates, or AI-assisted workflows.
- Run quality-control checks on data, metadata, and pairing logic.
- Use modern data tools, AI assistants, and chatbots productively to
  improve efficiency and reduce manual work.

#### Cross-Functional Collaboration

- Collaborate closely with clinicians, pathologists, and the data team
  to improve case selection, pairing logic, and dataset usability.
- Work in compliance with privacy, IRB, and site-specific data handling
  requirements.

### Qualifications

#### Educational Background

- Students only: 3rd year and above in Medicine, 3rd or 4th year B.Sc.
  students, or M.Sc. students in Life Sciences, Biology, Medical or
  Health Sciences, Bioinformatics or Biomedical Engineering.

#### Experience

- Experience working with structured datasets, metadata, or operational
  data workflows.
- Experience reading or working with clinical, biological, radiology, or
  pathology-related information.

#### Technical Skills

- Comfort working hands-on with structured data, metadata tables, and
  repetitive workflows.
- Practical experience with at least some of the following: **SQL,
  Python, spreadsheets with advanced formulas or logic, notebooks, or
  simple automation tools**.
- Confidence using **AI assistants, chatbots, or LLM tools**
  productively to accelerate technical tasks, troubleshoot, and identify
  the right tools for the job.
- Ability to reason systematically about data and apply logic to
  optimize data quality and usability.

#### Soft Skills

- Strong attention to detail and a highly structured way of thinking.
- Ability to read and summarize clinical information and use it to guide
  annotation decisions.
- Strong documentation and QA mindset.
- Ability to work independently while collaborating closely with
  technical and clinical stakeholders.
